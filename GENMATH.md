### Key Rules for Premium and Overtime Pay

1. **Night Differential Pay**
   * **Applicable Hours:** 10:00 p.m. to 6:00 a.m.
   * **Rate:** Additional 10% of the hourly rate (Multiplier: **1.1**)
   * **Formula:** Night Differential Pay = $1.1 \times \text{hourly rate}$

2. **Rest Day Pay**
   * **Applicable Days:** Scheduled rest day (minimum of 24 consecutive hours of rest after 6 consecutive workdays).
   * **Rate:** Additional 30% of the hourly rate (Multiplier: **1.3**)
   * **Formula:** Rest Day Pay = $1.3 \times \text{hourly rate}$

3. **Rest Day + Night Shift**
   * **Rate:** Additional 30% on top of the night differential rate (Multiplier: **1.43**)
   * **Formula:** Night Differential Pay (Rest Day) = $1.43 \times \text{hourly rate}$

4. **Overtime (OT) Multipliers**
   * **Regular Day OT:** Hourly rate $\times$ **1.25** $\times$ OT Hours
   * **Rest Day or Special Day OT:** Hourly rate $\times$ **1.69** $\times$ OT Hours
   * **Regular Holiday OT:** Hourly rate $\times$ **2.6** $\times$ OT Hours
   * **Regular Holiday on a Rest Day OT:** Hourly rate $\times$ **3.38** $\times$ OT Hours

### Application to the Presentation Examples

Here is how the entitlements apply to the specific scenarios listed in the presentation:

#### **Example 1: Security Guard (9:00 p.m. to 7:00 a.m.)**
* **Hourly Rate:** ₱115
* **Work Duration:** 10 hours total (8 regular hours + 2 overtime hours)
* **Breakdown of Earnings:**
  * **Regular Hours (9:00 p.m. to 5:00 a.m. = 8 hours):**
    * *9:00 p.m. – 10:00 p.m. (1 hour regular rate):* $1 \times \text{₱115} = \text{₱115.00}$
    * *10:00 p.m. – 5:00 a.m. (7 hours with Night Differential):* $7 \times (\text{₱115} \times 1.1) = \text{₱885.50}$
  * **Overtime Hours (5:00 a.m. to 7:00 a.m. = 2 hours):**
    * *5:00 a.m. – 6:00 a.m. (1 hour OT with Night Differential):* $1 \times \text{₱115} \times 1.25 \times 1.1 = \text{₱158.13}$
    * *6:00 a.m. – 7:00 a.m. (1 hour OT, regular daytime):* $1 \times \text{₱115} \times 1.25 = \text{₱143.75}$
  * **Total Daily Earnings:** $\text{₱115.00} + \text{₱885.50} + \text{₱158.13} + \text{₱143.75} = \mathbf{\text{₱1,302.38}}$

#### **Example 2: Gas Station Attendant (4:00 p.m. to 12:00 a.m. on a Double Holiday)**
* **Hourly Rate:** ₱135
* **Work Duration:** 8 hours
* **Double Holiday Premium:** Under standard Philippine labor guidelines, working on a double holiday yields 300% of the basic rate.
  * **Regular Hours (4:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m. = 6 hours):** 
    * $6 \text{ hours} \times (\text{₱135} \times 3.0) = \text{₱2,430.00}$
  * **Night Shift Hours (10:00 p.m. to 12:00 a.m. = 2 hours):**
    * $2 \text{ hours} \times (\text{₱135} \times 3.0 \times 1.1) = \text{₱891.00}$
  * **Total Daily Earnings:** $\text{₱2,430.00} + \text{₱891.00} = \mathbf{\text{₱3,321.00}}$

#### **Example 3: Carlo's Weekly Overtime Pay**
* **Hourly Rate:** ₱120
* **Day 1 (2 hours OT on a regular day):**
  * $\text{₱120} \times 1.25 \times 2 = \text{₱300.00}$
* **Day 3 (3 hours OT on a rest day):**
  * $\text{₱120} \times 1.69 \times 3 = \text{₱608.40}$
* **Day 5 (1.5 hours OT on a regular holiday falling on a rest day):**
  * $\text{₱120} \times 3.38 \times 1.5 = \text{₱608.40}$
* **Carlo’s Total Overtime Pay:** $\text{₱300.00} + \text{₱608.40} + \text{₱608.40} = \mathbf{\text{₱1,516.80}}$
